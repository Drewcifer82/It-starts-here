Common Questions

Wedding Limo FAQ — Springfield MO

How much does a wedding limo cost in Springfield MO? +
Wedding limo pricing in Springfield MO varies based on the vehicle you choose, the number of hours, your wedding date, and your specific route. Contact us at 417-865-5600 for a free personalized quote. We're transparent about pricing with no hidden fees.
How far in advance should I book a wedding limo in Springfield MO? +
We strongly recommend booking your wedding limo at least 3–6 months in advance, especially for spring and summer weddings. May, June, and October dates fill up the fastest. Don't risk losing your preferred vehicle — contact us as early as possible to secure your date.
What vehicles do you offer for weddings in Springfield MO? +
We offer three stunning wedding vehicles: The Executive (Chrysler 300 luxury limo, up to 8 passengers), Pinkey (the only pink limousine in the 417 area, up to 10 passengers), and the H2 Thunder Hummer limo for larger wedding parties (up to 14 passengers).
Do you serve wedding venues outside of Springfield MO? +
Absolutely. Unique Rides Transportation serves Springfield MO and the surrounding 300-mile region, including Branson, Joplin, Nixa, Ozark, Republic, the Lake of the Ozarks, and beyond. Wherever your venue is, we can get you there in style.
Can you decorate the limo for our wedding? +
Yes! We can coordinate custom decorations and personalized touches to match your wedding theme. Mention this when you book and we'll discuss the options to make your limo feel as special as your day.
Are your chauffeurs professional and background checked? +
Yes. Every Unique Rides Transportation chauffeur is professionally trained, background checked, and immaculately dressed. Our team is rooted in Christian values and treats your wedding with the respect and care it deserves.
