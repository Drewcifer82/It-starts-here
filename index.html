About This Dream Journal & Occult Sanctuary

Who walks this threshold

I have kept a dream journal for many years. What began as curiosity — scrawling half-remembered images at dawn — became something far deeper: a practice, a discipline, and eventually an initiation into the Western magical tradition.

This site is my open grimoire. Everything here is drawn from lived experience — the techniques that worked, the visions that changed me, and the old books that unlocked what the modern world forgot.
